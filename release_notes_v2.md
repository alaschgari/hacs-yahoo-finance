### Major New Features
- **Portfolio Tracking:** Enter `SYMBOL:AMOUNT` (e.g., AAPL:10) in the configuration to track holding values and total portfolio value.
- **Smart Polling (Eco-Mode):** Automatic detection of off-market hours and weekends. The integration slows down to a 10-minute interval during these times to save energy and API hits.
- **Technical Indicators:** Added 50-day and 200-day moving average sensors.
- **Enhanced Data:** News headlines, Dividend yield, and Next Earnings date are now fetched in a 6-hour background cycle.
- **Diversification:** Added 'Portfolio Weight' sensor to see how much each holding contributes to your total wealth.
- **Full Localization:** Updated English and German translations for all new features.
